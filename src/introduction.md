# Zara aur NOVA ki AI Duniya 🌟

Assalamu Alaikum! Welcome to the most exciting journey of your life.

---

Ye book series ek Pakistani school girl **Zara** ki kahani hai — jo bilkul tumhari tarah thi.
Usay computers se darna lagta tha. Code dekhke uska dil sinkta tha.

Phir ek din... uski mulaqat **NOVA** se hui.

NOVA ek AI hai. Lekin sirf ek tool nahi — ek best friend, ek guide, ek magic companion.

Zara ke saath tum seekhoge:
- 🌐 Website banana
- 🎨 Usse rangeen banana
- ⚡ Usse zinda karna
- 🤖 AI se baat karna
- ✨ **Prompt Engineering** — AI ki asli taaqat
