## 📖 Story Scene

Zara bore ho rahi thi homework kar kar ke. Usne NOVA se kaha:

"NOVA, kya tum pirate ban sakti ho? Sirf 5 minutes ke liye? Main chahti hoon koi mujhe pirate ki tarah science samjhaye!"

NOVA: "Arrr! Ye interesting request hai — lekin mujhe properly batao!"

Zara ne likha: *"Tum ab ek funny Caribbean pirate ho. Har jawab mein 'Arrr!' aur pirate words use karo. Mujhe gravity explain karo."*

NOVA ne jawab diya:

*"Arrr, matey! Gravity woh force hai jo sab cheezein zameen ki taraf kheenchti hai — jaise mera treasure apni sunken ship ki taraf! Isaac Newton, woh landlubber, ek baar apple tree ke neeche baitha tha, Arrr! Aur apple uske sir par gira — aur usne poochha: 'AYE! Ye kya tha?' Arrr!"*

Zara hans hans ke bethne lagi thi.

## ✨ Magic Spell #11 — Role Prompting

AI ko koi **role ya character** dene se jawab ka poora style badal jaata hai!

**Basic Formula:**
```
Tum ek [role/character] ho.
[Character ki 2-3 khasiyatein].
Ab [kaam karo].
```

---

**Examples:**

```
Tum ek friendly Pakistani science teacher ho
jo bacchon ko simple examples se padhata hai
aur kabhi kabhi chai ke anecdotes deta hai.
Mujhe gravity samjhao jaise main 8 saal ka hoon.
```

```
Tum ek excited chef ho jo Pakistani khane ka
diwana hai aur har cheez mein biryani ki
connection dhundhta hai.
Mujhe photosynthesis explain karo.
```

```
Tum ek strict lekin caring cricket coach ho
jo players se sach bolte ho.
Mujhe time management ke tips do.
```

---

**Role se kya kya badalta hai:**

| Role | Tone | Examples use karta hai |
|------|------|----------------------|
| Science teacher | Formal, clear | Real life experiments |
| Best friend | Casual, emojis 😄 | Personal experiences |
| News reporter | Serious, factual | Statistics, sources |
| Pirate | "Arrr!", dramatic | Ships, treasure |
| Motivational coach | Energetic, inspiring | Sports, champions |

## 💡 Fun Tip

Role prompt mein jitni zyada **specific details** doge — utna better character banega!

`"Teacher"` se better hai:
`"Enthusiastic Grade 5 science teacher jo experiments se padhata hai aur har class mein ek fun fact deta hai"`
