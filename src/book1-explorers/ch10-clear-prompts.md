## 📖 Story Scene

Zara ne NOVA se kaha: "Meri school friend Ali ke liye birthday message likho."

NOVA ne likha:

*"Dear Ali, Happy Birthday! Wishing you all the best on your special day. May all your dreams come true. Best wishes!"*

Zara ne socha — ye toh bilkul boring aur generic hai!

"NOVA, ye achha nahi hai!"

NOVA: "Zara, tumne mujhe bataya nahi ke Ali kaisa hai, tum dono ki friendship kaisi hai, tone kya chahiye, Ali ki koi special cheez. Main kya karoon? Mind reading nahi aati mujhe!"

Zara ko samajh aaya — apna prompt zyada clear karna padega!

---

## ✨ Magic Spell #10 — Clear Prompt Techniques

**Technique 1: Context do**

```
❌ Ali ke liye birthday message
✅ Mera best friend Ali cricket ka diwana hai.
   Hum dono school ki cricket team mein hain.
   Uske liye ek funny birthday message likho
   jo cricket jokes se bhara ho.
   Casual tone, 3-4 lines, Roman Urdu mein.
```

---

**Technique 2: Format clearly batao**

```
❌ mujhe list do
✅ Mujhe 5 points ki numbered list do.
   Har point sirf 1 sentence ka ho.
   Simple words use karo.
```

---

**Technique 3: Examples do — AI seekhega (Few-Shot)**

```
✅ Mujhe fun facts do exactly iss format mein:
   "Kya tum jaante ho ke [fact]!"

   Jaise:
   "Kya tum jaante ho ke octopus ke 3 dil hote hain!"
   "Kya tum jaante ho ke giraffes ki tongue kaali hoti hai!"

   Ab 3 aur aise facts do Pakistan ke baare mein.
```

AI example dekh ke exact same format follow karta hai!

---

**Technique 4: Role do AI ko**

```
✅ Tum ek friendly science teacher ho
   jo bacchon ko simple examples se padhata hai.
   Mujhe atoms samjhao jaise main 8 saal ka hoon.
   Ek real-life example zaroor do.
```

---

**Kya avoid karen:**

| Weak word | Better alternative |
|-----------|-------------------|
| "kuch batao" | "5 points batao" |
| "achha jawab do" | "formal tone mein, 100 words mein" |
| "help karo" | "mujhe [specific kaam] mein help karo" |
| "likhdo" | "Roman Urdu mein, 3 paragraphs mein likhdo" |
