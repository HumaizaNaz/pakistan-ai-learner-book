# Humari Characters 🎭

## Zara 👧

**Kaun hai:** Ek Pakistani school girl — Karachi mein rehti hai.

**Personality:**
- Curious hai lekin kabhi kabhi dar jaati hai
- Galtiyan karti hai — aur unse seekhti hai
- Dosto ki madad karna chahti hai
- Cricket aur biryani ka bohot shauq hai

**Journey:** Jab story shuru hoti hai, Zara computers se darti hai. Har book mein thodi aur brave, aur zyada skilled hoti jaati hai.

---

## NOVA 🤖✨

**Kaun hai:** Zara ki AI best friend. Claude-powered. Zara ke phone mein rehti hai.

**Personality:**
- Smart aur patient — kabhi frustrated nahi hoti
- Kabhi kabhi confusing jawab deti hai — **JAB ZARA KA PROMPT GALAT HO**
- Humor sense hai — jokes karti hai
- Honestly batati hai jab kuch nahi pata

**Important Rule:** NOVA perfect nahi hai. Jab Zara clearly nahi bolti, NOVA galat ya funny jawab deti hai. Ye intentional hai — students naturally seekhte hain ke clear prompts kyun zaroori hain.

| Situation | NOVA kya karti hai |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Clear prompt | Perfect helpful jawab |
| Vague prompt | Confusing/wrong jawab |
| Funny prompt | Unexpectedly funny response |
| Complex question | Step by step explain karti hai |
