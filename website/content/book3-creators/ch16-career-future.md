**Ethical AI Developer banna:**

```
Power ke saath responsibility aati hai:

1. TRUTHFULNESS
   AI hallucinations ke baare mein users ko batao.
   Capabilities aur limitations honest rakho.

2. PRIVACY
   User data carefully handle karo.
   Jo zaroori nahi — collect mat karo.

3. FAIRNESS
   AI bias check karo — different groups ke liye
   equally well kaam kare.

4. SAFETY
   Harmful use cases prevent karo.
   Safeguards build karo.

5. TRANSPARENCY
   Users ko batao ke AI use ho rahi hai.
```
