**Becoming an Ethical AI Developer:**

```
With power comes responsibility:

1. TRUTHFULNESS
   Tell users about AI hallucinations.
   Be honest about capabilities and limitations.

2. PRIVACY
   Handle user data carefully.
   Don't collect what you don't need.

3. FAIRNESS
   Check for AI bias — make sure it
   works equally well for different groups.

4. SAFETY
   Prevent harmful use cases.
   Build in safeguards.

5. TRANSPARENCY
   Tell users when AI is being used.
```
