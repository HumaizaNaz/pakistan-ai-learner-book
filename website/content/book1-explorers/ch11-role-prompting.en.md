## 📖 Story Scene

Zara was getting bored doing homework. She said to NOVA:

"NOVA, can you be a pirate? Just for 5 minutes? I want someone to explain science to me in pirate style!"

NOVA: "Arrr! That's an interesting request — but you have to tell me properly!"

Zara wrote: *"You are now a funny Caribbean pirate. Use 'Arrr!' and pirate words in every answer. Explain gravity to me."*

NOVA replied:

*"Arrr, matey! Gravity be the force that pulls all things toward the ground — just like me treasure pulls toward me sunken ship! Isaac Newton, that landlubber, was once sitting under an apple tree, Arrr! And an apple fell on his head — and he asked: 'AYE! What was that?' Arrr!"*

Zara was laughing so hard she could barely stay in her chair.

## ✨ Magic Spell #11 — Role Prompting

Giving AI a **role or character** completely changes the style of its answer!

**Basic Formula:**
```
You are a [role/character].
[2-3 traits of the character].
Now [do the task].
```

---

**Examples:**

```
You are a friendly Pakistani science teacher
who teaches children using simple examples
and occasionally brings in chai anecdotes.
Explain gravity to me like I'm 8 years old.
```

```
You are an excited chef who is obsessed
with Pakistani food and finds a biryani
connection in everything.
Explain photosynthesis to me.
```

```
You are a strict but caring cricket coach
who tells players the truth.
Give me time management tips.
```

---

**What changes with a role:**

| Role | Tone | Examples used |
|------|------|--------------|
| Science teacher | Formal, clear | Real life experiments |
| Best friend | Casual, emojis 😄 | Personal experiences |
| News reporter | Serious, factual | Statistics, sources |
| Pirate | "Arrr!", dramatic | Ships, treasure |
| Motivational coach | Energetic, inspiring | Sports, champions |

## 💡 Fun Tip

The more **specific details** you add to a role prompt — the better the character becomes!

`"Teacher"` is weaker than:
`"An enthusiastic Grade 5 science teacher who teaches through experiments and gives one fun fact every class"`
