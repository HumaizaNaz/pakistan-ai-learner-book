## 📖 Story Scene

Zara told NOVA: "Write a birthday message for my school friend Ali."

NOVA wrote:

*"Dear Ali, Happy Birthday! Wishing you all the best on your special day. May all your dreams come true. Best wishes!"*

Zara thought — this is completely boring and generic!

"NOVA, this isn't good!"

NOVA: "Zara, you didn't tell me what Ali is like, what your friendship is like, what tone you wanted, anything special about Ali. What am I supposed to do? I can't read minds!"

Zara understood — she needed to make her prompts a lot clearer!

---

## ✨ Magic Spell #10 — Clear Prompt Techniques

**Technique 1: Give Context**

```
❌ Birthday message for Ali
✅ My best friend Ali is obsessed with cricket.
   We're both on our school cricket team.
   Write him a funny birthday message
   packed with cricket jokes.
   Casual tone, 3-4 lines, in English.
```

---

**Technique 2: State the Format Clearly**

```
❌ give me a list
✅ Give me a numbered list of 5 points.
   Each point should be just 1 sentence.
   Use simple words.
```

---

**Technique 3: Give Examples — AI Will Learn (Few-Shot)**

```
✅ Give me fun facts in exactly this format:
   "Did you know that [fact]!"

   Like:
   "Did you know that octopuses have 3 hearts!"
   "Did you know that giraffes have black tongues!"

   Now give me 3 more facts like these about Pakistan.
```

AI sees the example and follows the exact same format!

---

**Technique 4: Give AI a Role**

```
✅ You are a friendly science teacher
   who teaches children with simple examples.
   Explain atoms to me like I'm 8 years old.
   Give at least one real-life example.
```

---

**What to avoid:**

| Weak phrase | Better alternative |
|-------------|-------------------|
| "tell me something" | "tell me 5 points" |
| "give a good answer" | "formal tone, in 100 words" |
| "help me" | "help me with [specific task]" |
| "write it" | "write it in English, in 3 paragraphs" |

## 💡 Fun Tip

Writing **"step by step"** in your prompt makes AI think more carefully. Try:
`"Explain step by step how photosynthesis works"` — and also try it without "step by step." Notice the difference!
