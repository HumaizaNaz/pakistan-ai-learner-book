## 📖 Story Scene

Miss Ayesha ne class assignment diya: "Apni class ke liye Pakistan history ka ek quiz banao — 10 questions. Kal submit karna hai."

Zara ne socha — ye toh ghanton ka kaam hai! Research karo, questions socho, options banao, answers check karo...

"NOVA, kya ye possible hai 5 minutes mein?"

"Ek achha prompt likho," NOVA ne kaha. "Aur dekhte hain."

Zara ne carefully ek prompt craft kiya. 45 seconds mein — poora quiz ready tha. Well-structured, interesting questions, 4 options each, answer key bhi.

Miss Ayesha ne quiz dekha. "Zara, ye bohot achha hai! Tumne bohot mehnat ki?"

Zara muskurai. "Haan... kuch zyada hi mehnat!" 😄

## ✨ Magic Spell #12 — Creative Prompt Templates

**1. Quiz Creator:**
```
[Subject] par 10 multiple choice questions banao.
Level: [Grade X ke liye]
Har question ke 4 options hon (A, B, C, D).
Sirf ek option correct ho.
End mein answer key alag se do.
Roman Urdu mein questions likho.
```

---

**2. Story Generator:**
```
Ek short story likho (250 words).
Setting: Pakistan ka ek chhota shahar, 2026 mein.
Main character: 12-saal ki ek curious girl.
Problem: Uska science fair project kaam nahi kar raha.
Ending: Positive, lesson ke saath.
Tone: Fun aur exciting.
```

---

**3. Poem Writer:**
```
[Topic] par ek 8-line poem:
- ABAB rhyme scheme (1 aur 3 rhyme, 2 aur 4 rhyme)
- [Mood/tone] ka tone
- Simple words — 10-saal ke bachche ke liye
- Roman Urdu mein
```

---

**4. Explainer for Kids:**
```
Mujhe [concept] explain karo jaise
main ek 10-saal ka curious bachcha hoon.
- 5 sentences mein rakho
- Ek funny real-life analogy use karo
- End mein ek wow fact do
```

---

**5. Lesson Plan (Teachers ke liye!):**
```
[Topic] par ek 45-minute class ka plan banao.
Class: Grade [X]
Include karo:
- 5 min: Warm-up activity
- 20 min: Main lesson
- 15 min: Practice exercise
- 5 min: Summary
Simple materials use karo (sirf whiteboard/paper).
```

---

## 🔥 Try It Yourself

Apni class ke liye koi bhi subject choose karo aur quiz banwao:

```
[Subject] par 5 multiple choice questions banao.
Har question ke 4 options hon (A, B, C, D).
[Class level] ke liye appropriate level.
Answer key end mein do.
Roman Urdu mein questions likho.
```

Phir apne dost ko de do solve karne ke liye!
